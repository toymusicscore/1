ToyScore

ToyScore is a musical score and a software keyboard at the sametime.
You can see note names, lylics, memos and fingering to set on the notes.
You can hear the sound of the notes when you tap the notes on your smartphone or tablet pc. You can learn how to play the piano by tracing the notes on the screen.
Try Demo to select demo songs.
Try Local to open your midi and text files.
