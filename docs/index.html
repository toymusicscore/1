ToyScore

ToyScore is a piano roll score on a software keyboard.

You can see note names, fingering, lylics, memos and more to set on the notes.
You can hear the sound of the notes when you tap the notes on your smartphone or tablet pc.
You can learn how to play the piano by tracing the notes on the screen.

Try Demo to select a demo song.
Try Local to open your local files.
