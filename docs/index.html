Pianorollscore

Pianorollscore is a musical score that looks like the piano keyboard and it's easy to find notes on the piano.

You can open your midi and text files together to show lylics, memos and fingering on pianorollscore.
You can also estimate the key of the song and draw geometric figures of chords in Pythagorean tuning and Just intonation.

Try Demo to select demo songs.
Try Local to open your midi and text files. You can download files of demo songs from "Download Demo".
